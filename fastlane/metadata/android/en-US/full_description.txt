RealmScore is a simple, fast score keeper for the card game Fantasy Realms.

Features:
- Track scores for 2 to 6 players
- Automatic calculation of all bonuses, penalties and wild-card effects
- Suspense mode: scores are revealed together at the end of a round
- Sandbox for freely experimenting with card combinations
- Detailed statistics about players and cards
- Fully offline, no ads, no trackers

RealmScore is an unofficial fan app and is not affiliated with the publishers of the game.
